# Market Intelligence Quiz 3
## Golden Visa & Residency

This quiz tests your understanding of Module 1.8: Golden Visa & Residency. You need 80% (4 out of 5 correct) to pass.

---

### Question 1

**What is the minimum property value required for Golden Visa eligibility through real estate investment?**

- [ ] A) AED 1 million
- [x] B) AED 2 million
- [ ] C) AED 3 million
- [ ] D) AED 5 million

**Explanation**: The Golden Visa property investment route requires a minimum property value of AED 2 million. This is assessed by RERA valuation, not purchase price.

---

### Question 2

**Can you obtain a Golden Visa immediately upon purchasing an off-plan property worth AED 3 million?**

- [ ] A) Yes, as long as the value exceeds AED 2 million
- [x] B) No, the property must be completed with title deed issued
- [ ] C) Yes, but only if you pay 100% upfront
- [ ] D) Yes, after six months of payments

**Explanation**: Golden Visa eligibility requires a completed property with title deed. Off-plan properties do not qualify until handover and title deed issuance, regardless of value.

---

### Question 3

**What is the duration of the UAE Golden Visa for property investors?**

- [ ] A) 5 years, renewable
- [x] B) 10 years, renewable
- [ ] C) Permanent residency
- [ ] D) 3 years, renewable

**Explanation**: The property investor Golden Visa provides 10-year residency that is renewable as long as you continue to meet the property ownership requirements.

---

### Question 4

**If you have a mortgage on your AED 3 million property, what is the maximum mortgage amount to still qualify for Golden Visa?**

- [ ] A) No mortgage allowed
- [ ] B) AED 500,000
- [ ] C) AED 1 million
- [x] D) AED 1.5 million (50% of property value)

**Explanation**: The mortgage must not exceed 50% of the property value. With a AED 3 million property, you could have up to AED 1.5 million mortgage and still qualify, as your equity would be AED 1.5 million and the property value exceeds AED 2 million.

---

### Question 5

**Which family members can a Golden Visa holder sponsor?**

- [ ] A) Spouse only
- [ ] B) Spouse and children under 18 only
- [x] C) Spouse, sons up to 25, and unmarried daughters of any age
- [ ] D) No family sponsorship is permitted

**Explanation**: Golden Visa holders can sponsor their spouse, sons up to age 25 (extended from 18), and unmarried daughters of any age. Parents can also be sponsored under certain conditions.
