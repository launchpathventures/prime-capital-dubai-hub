## Process Overview

1. **Trainee completes learning** — All modules and quizzes in the LMS
2. **Trainee reviews scenarios** — Practices with AI using prompts in `/learn/scenarios`
3. **Trainee schedules session** — Contacts founding team offline
4. **Evaluator conducts assessment** — In-person, 75-90 minutes, recorded
5. **Evaluator scores and decides** — Uses rubric, records in LMS admin
6. **Outcome communicated** — Within 48 hours

## Scenario Sets

**Set A (Primary)** — Used for first attempts
- DS-01: End-User Buyer
- OB-01: Bubble Concern
- PR-01: First Property View
- CL-01: EOI Commitment
- DIF-03: Developer Delay

**Set B (Alternate)** — Used for retakes
- DS-02: Pure Investor
- OB-03: Hidden Fees Concern
- CL-02: Final Hesitation
- FC-01: Portal Lead Response
- DIF-02: Lost Deal to Competitor

## Passing Criteria

- Average rubric score ≥ 3.5
- No individual dimension below 2
- Overall impression: Ready to represent Prime Capital
