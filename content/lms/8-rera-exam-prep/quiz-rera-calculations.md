# RERA Calculations & Valuation Quiz

- **Passing Score:** 75% (12/15 correct)

**Note:** Calculator use recommended. Remember key conversion factors:
- 1 sq ft = 0.0929 sq m
- 1 sq m = 10.764 sq ft

---

## Question 1

A property is sold for AED 2,750,000. Calculate the total DLD registration fee (4%):

- [ ] AED 55,000
- [ ] AED 82,500
- [x] AED 110,000
- [ ] AED 137,500

**Explanation:** DLD fee = AED 2,750,000 × 4% = AED 2,750,000 × 0.04 = AED 110,000. This fee is typically split between buyer and seller (AED 55,000 each).

- [ ] AED 1,900
- [x] AED 4,750

**Explanation:** Rental commission = AED 95,000 × 5% = AED 4,750. This is the standard rental commission rate in Dubai for residential leasing.

## Question 3

Convert 1,850 square feet to square meters:

- [ ] 154.2 sq m
- [x] 171.9 sq m
- [ ] 199.1 sq m
- [ ] 19,911 sq m

**Explanation:** 1,850 sq ft × 0.0929 = 171.87 sq m ≈ 171.9 sq m. Remember: sq ft to sq m, multiply by 0.0929.

## Question 4

A property purchased for AED 1,450,000 generates annual rent of AED 98,000. What is the gross rental yield?

- [ ] 5.86%
- [ ] 6.21%
- [x] 6.76%
- [ ] 7.13%

**Explanation:** Gross yield = (Annual Rent / Purchase Price) × 100 = (98,000 / 1,450,000) × 100 = 6.76%.

## Question 8

A property has annual rent of AED 75,000 and annual expenses (service charge, maintenance) of AED 12,000. Purchase price was AED 950,000. What is the net rental yield?

- [ ] 6.32%
- [x] 6.63%
- [ ] 7.89%
- [ ] 9.16%

**Explanation:** Net yield = (Annual Rent - Expenses) / Purchase Price × 100 = (75,000 - 12,000) / 950,000 × 100 = 63,000 / 950,000 × 100 = 6.63%.

## Question 12

## Question 14

A property generates monthly rent of AED 8,500. Calculate the annual rent and gross yield if purchase price was AED 1,150,000:

- [ ] Annual rent: AED 85,000, Yield: 7.39%
- [x] Annual rent: AED 102,000, Yield: 8.87%
- [ ] Annual rent: AED 102,000, Yield: 7.39%
- [ ] Annual rent: AED 8,500, Yield: 0.74%

**Explanation:** Annual rent = AED 8,500 × 12 = AED 102,000. Gross yield = (102,000 / 1,150,000) × 100 = 8.87%.

## Question 15

A property transaction involves: Purchase price AED 2,400,000, DLD fee 4% (split 50/50), broker commission 2% (buyer's share 1%), admin fee AED 580. What is the buyer's total transaction cost (excluding the property price)?

- [ ] AED 96,580
- [ ] AED 24,000
- [x] AED 72,580
- [ ] AED 120,580

**Explanation:** Buyer's costs: DLD share (50% of 4% = 2% of property value) = AED 2,400,000 × 2% = AED 48,000 + Commission share (1% of property value) = AED 24,000 + Admin fee = AED 580. Total = AED 48,000 + AED 24,000 + AED 580 = AED 72,580.

## Key Formulas to Remember

- **Gross Yield:** (Annual Rent / Purchase Price) × 100
- **Net Yield:** (Annual Rent - Expenses) / Purchase Price × 100
- **DLD Fee:** Property Value × 4%
- **Sq ft to Sq m:** × 0.0929
- **Sq m to Sq ft:** × 10.764
- **Security Deposit (Unfurnished):** Annual Rent × 5%
- **Security Deposit (Furnished):** Annual Rent × 10%
