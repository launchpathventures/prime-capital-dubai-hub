## Flow

1. **Frame** — What kind of planning (first version, new feature, promotion)
2. **Questions** — Core planning questions based on type
3. **Plan** — Propose milestones and workstreams
4. **Briefs** — Convert plan to briefs (optional)
5. **Summary** — Show next steps
