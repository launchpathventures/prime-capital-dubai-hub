## Process

1. **Parse feedback** — Extract issues from review
2. **Prioritise** — Critical first, then important
3. **Fix** — Implement each fix, verify it addresses the feedback
4. **Summarise** — Show what was done, what was deferred
5. **Recommend** — Ready to ship or needs another review loop
