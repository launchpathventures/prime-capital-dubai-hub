# Market Intelligence

## Competency Overview

As a Prime Capital advisor, your credibility begins with deep market knowledge. International investors—many conducting their first Dubai transaction—need confidence that you understand the market better than they do. This competency equips you with the foundational intelligence to earn that confidence.

## Why This Matters

Dubai's real estate market recorded **169,083 transactions in 2024**, a 42% increase year-over-year, with total value reaching **AED 427 billion**. This growth attracts sophisticated investors worldwide, but it also raises their due diligence standards. They will test your knowledge.

The Prime Capital difference is expertise without pressure. You don't sell properties—you provide intelligence that enables informed decisions. This competency builds the knowledge foundation that makes that positioning authentic.

### Learning Path

**Foundation First**: Start with modules 1.1-1.3 to build core market understanding. These provide the factual foundation for client conversations.

**Practical Application**: Modules 1.2, 1.4, 1.8, and 1.10 include Coach Walkthrough sections with scripts and practice scenarios. These connect knowledge to real client situations.

**Deep Expertise**: Modules 1.5-1.7 provide specialist knowledge on developers and areas. Reference these when matching properties to client requirements.

**Strategic Context**: Modules 1.9-1.10 position Dubai within the global investment landscape—essential for international clients evaluating alternatives.

## Key Statistics to Know

These numbers will appear frequently in client conversations:

- **Market Volume**: AED 427 billion in 2024 transactions
- **Transaction Growth**: 42% YoY increase (169,083 sales)
- **Average Yield**: 6.9% rental returns
- **Golden Visa**: AED 2 million property threshold
- **Freehold Areas**: 40+ designated zones for international buyers
- **Escrow Protection**: 100% of off-plan payments held in regulated accounts
