# Competency 6: Objection Navigation

## Overview

This competency teaches Real Estate Consultants to handle investor objections professionally using evidence-based responses, the acknowledge-before-address framework, and strategic follow-up techniques.

## Modules

### 6.1 - Objection Handling Framework (Skills)
**Duration:** 30 min  
**Learning Objective:** Navigate investor objections using the acknowledge-before-address framework with confidence

**Key Content:**
- Five objection types: Need, Understanding, Trust, Shortcoming, Tactical
- The acknowledge-before-address rule (critical technique)
- Coach walkthrough with dialogue examples using `<speak>` tags
- Objection response formula (3-step process)
- AI Practice simulation with Sarah (skeptical UK investor)

**Quiz:** objection-navigation-1

---

### 6.2 - Market & Timing Objections (Skills + Script)
**Duration:** 35 min  
**Learning Objective:** Address market bubble concerns and timing objections with Dubai-specific data and evidence

**Key Content:**
- "Is Dubai a bubble?" objection with coach walkthrough
- 2024 DLD data: 169,083 transactions, AED 427 billion value
- Historical context: 2008 vs 2024 market structure comparison
- Cost-of-waiting analysis with specific examples
- Response scripts for common variations (market crash fears, oversupply concerns)

**Quiz:** objection-navigation-2

---

### 6.3 - Developer & Project Objections (Skills + Script)
**Duration:** 30 min  
**Learning Objective:** Defuse developer reliability and construction delay concerns using track record data and regulatory protections

**Key Content:**
- Five developer objection categories
- Coach walkthrough on construction delay concerns
- Developer tier system with delivery track records (Emaar: 81% on-time, DAMAC: 76%, etc.)
- RERA escrow protection explanation with milestone-based releases
- Response scripts for developer trust and quality concerns

---

### 6.4 - Price & Fee Objections (Skills + Script)
**Duration:** 30 min  
**Learning Objective:** Convert price and hidden fee objections into transparent cost breakdowns that build trust

**Key Content:**
- Complete Dubai transaction cost breakdown (6-7% total)
- Annual ownership costs: AED 30-40K (service charges, chiller, DEWA)
- Coach walkthrough on hidden fees concern with detailed spreadsheet
- Response scripts for relative price objections and negotiation tactics
- Zero property tax, capital gains tax, or inheritance tax advantages

---

### 6.5 - Stall & Delay Objections (Skills + Script)
**Duration:** 25 min  
**Learning Objective:** Reframe stall tactics and delay objections to uncover real concerns and maintain momentum

**Key Content:**
- Five types of stall objections (polite brush-off, information overload, hidden objection, comparison shopping, timing/authority)
- Coach walkthrough on "I need to think about it" stall with diagnostic questioning
- Diagnostic question framework to uncover hidden objections
- Response scripts for spouse discussion, timing delays, and comparison shopping stalls

---

### 6.6 - Strategic Follow-Up (Skills)
**Duration:** 25 min  
**Learning Objective:** Maintain prospect engagement through value-add follow-up that reframes objections over time

**Key Content:**
- Follow-up purpose hierarchy (5 levels from requested info to requalification)
- Timing rules by lead temperature (hot: 48 hours, warm: 1 week, cool: 2 weeks)
- Value-add follow-up examples addressing specific objections
- The polite close-out requalification message
- CRM tracking templates

---

### 6.7 - Failure Scenarios & Resilience (Skills)
**Duration:** 25 min  
**Learning Objective:** Maintain professional composure and extract learning from failed objection handling scenarios

**Key Content:**
- Five common objection handling failure types
- The 10/10/10 rule for processing lost deals (10 min feel, 10 hours learn, 10 days move on)
- Failure learning log template
- Conversion rate benchmarks (10-20% inquiry to sale is normal)
- When to walk away professionally
- Objection handling debrief template

**Quiz:** objection-navigation-3
