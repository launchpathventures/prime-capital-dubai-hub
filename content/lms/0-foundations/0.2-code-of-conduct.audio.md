## Communication Standards: The Checklist

[PRACTICE TIP]

Before you send ANY client communicationâ€”email, WhatsApp, voice noteâ€”run through this checklist:

**Timing:**
- Is this during reasonable business hours (9 AM - 7 PM Dubai time)?
- If it's outside hours, is this genuinely urgent or pre-arranged?

**Tone:**
- Would I use this language in a meeting with my own financial advisor?
- Is the tone calm and professional, not excited or urgent?

**Content:**
- Am I providing value (information, context, analysis) or just pushing inventory?
- Have I used specific details instead of vague superlatives?
- Have I respected what the client told me about their preferences?

**Format:**
- Is this the right channel? (Email for detailed information, WhatsApp for quick coordination)
- Have I proofread for typos and grammar?
- Have I minimized or eliminated emojis?

**Response expectations:**
- Am I giving them space to respond in their own time?
- Have I avoided double-messaging or "just checking in" follow-ups?

If you can't answer yes to all of these, revise before sending.
