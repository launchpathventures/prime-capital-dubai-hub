# Certification Assessment ‚Äî Evaluator Guide
## Prime Capital Consultant Readiness Assessment

### Purpose

This guide helps founding team members conduct consistent, fair, and effective
certification assessments. The goal is not to "test" candidates but to 
validate they're ready to represent Prime Capital to prospective clients.

### Philosophy

[Explain the assessment philosophy]
- Conversation not examination
- Looking for readiness, not perfection
- Brand alignment matters as much as knowledge
- Create a safe space for authentic performance

### Before the Session

Checklist:
- Review learner's progress in LMS (modules completed, quiz scores)
- Note any areas of concern from their learning journey
- Prepare recording equipment
- Allocate 75-90 minutes uninterrupted
- Have this guide and rubric accessible

### Session Structure

1. **Welcome & Setup** (5 min)
   - Put candidate at ease
   - Explain the format
   - Confirm recording consent
   - Set expectations

2. **Scenario Roleplay** (50-60 min)
   - Run through 5 fixed scenarios
   - 10-12 minutes each
   - You play the client persona
   - Stay in character, but be fair
   - Note observations after each

3. **Debrief & Discussion** (10-15 min)
   - Ask how they felt it went
   - Discuss any scenarios they found challenging
   - Share initial impressions
   - Do NOT give pass/fail decision in the moment

4. **Post-Session** (15 min, alone)
   - Complete rubric scoring
   - Write qualitative notes
   - Make pass/needs-development decision
   - Record outcome in platform

### Playing the Client Personas

[Guidance on roleplay]
- Commit to the persona ‚Äî use their concerns, style, priorities
- Don't make it artificially difficult
- Give realistic buying signals when appropriate
- If candidate is struggling, stay in character but create openings
- Your job is assessment, not stress testing

### Decision Framework

Pass when:
- Average rubric score ‚â• 3.5
- No dimension below 2
- Overall impression: "I'd be comfortable with them representing us"

Needs Development when:
- Significant gaps in market knowledge
- Brand tone misalignment (too pushy, too passive)
- Process confusion that could cause client problems
- Unable to handle standard objections confidently

### Giving Feedback

- Wait at least 24 hours before delivering outcome
- Lead with strengths observed
- Be specific about development areas
- Provide clear path forward if not passed
- Offer to schedule follow-up session

### Recording & Documentation

- Use [recording tool/platform]
- Upload recording to [location]
- Complete outcome form in LMS admin
- Include specific examples in notes

### 3. Trainee Preparation Guide

**File:** `content/lms/certification/trainee-guide.md`

**Content Structure:**

```markdown
# Preparing for Your Certification Assessment
## What to Expect and How to Succeed

### What This Is

Your certification assessment is a conversation with one of Prime Capital's
founding team. It's not an exam ‚Äî it's a practical demonstration that you're
ready to represent the firm and serve prospective clients.

Think of it as a supportive final check before you begin client-facing work.

### Format

- **Duration:** 60-75 minutes
- **Structure:** 5 roleplay scenarios
- **Setting:** In-person with your evaluator
- **Recording:** The session will be recorded for quality purposes

### The Scenarios

You'll work through these five scenarios:

1. **Discovery: End-User Buyer**
   A family relocating to Dubai needs help finding a home. Test your needs
   analysis and listening skills.

2. **Objection: Bubble Concern**
   An analytical investor worries about market timing. Test your market
   knowledge and ability to address concerns with data.

3. **Presentation: First Property Viewing**
   Guide a client through their first property tour. Test your product
   knowledge and ability to tell the story.

4. **Closing: EOI Commitment**
   A ready buyer needs guidance through the next steps. Test your process
   expertise and ability to facilitate decisions.

5. **Difficult: Frustrated Client**
   A client is upset about a developer delay. Test your composure and
   ability to de-escalate professionally.

### What Evaluators Are Looking For

**Market Knowledge**
Do you understand Dubai real estate well enough to advise confidently?

**Client Focus**
Do you listen more than you talk? Do you uncover what clients really need?

**Communication**
Are you clear, confident, and aligned with Prime Capital's professional tone?

**Process Expertise**
Can you guide clients through the transaction process with authority?

**Composure**
Can you handle pressure, objections, and difficult moments gracefully?

### How to Prepare

**Review your modules**
Revisit any areas where you scored lower on quizzes. Focus on fundamentals.

**Practice the scenarios**
Use the AI roleplay prompts in the Practice Scenarios section. The more
you practice, the more natural you'll feel.

**Know your weaknesses**
Be honest about areas where you're less confident. It's better to acknowledge
a gap than to bluff through it.

**Get sleep**
This isn't a stress test, but you'll perform better rested.

### During the Session

**Be yourself**
We're looking for authenticity, not performance. Your natural style is fine ‚Äî
we just want to see it applied professionally.

**Listen carefully**
Pay attention to what the "client" (your evaluator) is saying. They're giving
you information and signals.

**It's okay to pause**
Take a moment to think if you need it. Thoughtful responses are better than
rushed ones.

**Ask questions**
If you're uncertain what the client means, ask. That's what a good consultant
does.

**Stay in character**
Treat each scenario as a real client conversation. Don't break character to
ask the evaluator questions ‚Äî handle it as you would in real life.

### After the Session

You won't receive your outcome immediately. The evaluator will:
- Review their notes
- Complete the formal assessment
- Contact you within 24-48 hours with results and feedback

### If You Need More Development

This isn't failure ‚Äî it's feedback. If areas for development are identified:
- You'll receive specific guidance on what to work on
- You can schedule another session when ready
- Many successful consultants needed more than one attempt

### Questions?

Contact [scheduling contact] to discuss any concerns before your session.
```
